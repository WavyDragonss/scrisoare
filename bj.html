Blackjack Game Rules

You play classic Blackjack against the dealer.

Objective: Try to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values:
- Number cards are worth their face value.
- Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points.
- Aces can count as 1 or 11 points, whichever benefits your hand most.

Your Turn:
- Hit: Draw a card.
- Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn.
After you stand, the dealer plays automatically, drawing cards until reaching at least 17 points.

Busting: If your hand value exceeds 21, you bust and lose the round.
If the dealer busts, you win the round.
If neither busts, the higher hand wins.

Progress:
- Winning 5 rounds unlocks the next step (you will be redirected).
- Losing 3 rounds resets the game and your progress.

Try to beat the dealer and advance to the surprise!
Good luck!
